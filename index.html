Azkar App

Morning Azkar

Subhanallah (Hausa: Tsarki ya tabbata ga Allah)

Arabic: سبحان الله

English: Glory be to Allah

Alhamdulillah (Hausa: Godiya ta tabbata ga Allah)

Arabic: الحمد لله

English: Praise be to Allah

Allahu Akbar (Hausa: Allah ya fi kowa girma)

Arabic: الله أكبر

English: Allah is the Greatest

La ilaha illallah (Hausa: Babu wani abin bautawa da ya cancanta sai Allah)

Arabic: لا إله إلا الله

English: There is no deity but Allah

Astaghfirullah (Hausa: Ina neman gafarar Allah)

Arabic: أستغفر الله

English: I seek forgiveness from Allah

La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah (Hausa: Babu ƙarfin aikata alheri sai tare da Allah)

Arabic: لا حول ولا قوة إلا بالله

English: There is no power or might except with Allah

Bismillah (Hausa: Da sunan Allah)

Arabic: بسم الله

English: In the name of Allah

Subhanallah wa bihamdihi (Hausa: Tsarki ya tabbata ga Allah da godiyarsa)

Arabic: سبحان الله وبحمده

English: Glory be to Allah and praise be to Him

Rabbi ghfirli (Hausa: Ya Ubangiji, ka gafarta mini)

Arabic: ربي اغفر لي

English: My Lord, forgive me

Rabbi zidni ilma (Hausa: Ya Ubangiji, ka ƙara mini ilimi)

Arabic: ربي زدني علما

English: My Lord, increase me in knowledge

Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa huwa (Hausa: Allah ya isar mini, babu abin bautawa sai shi)

Arabic: حسبي الله لا إله إلا هو

English: Allah is sufficient for me; there is no deity except Him

Subhanallah wa bihamdihi, Subhanallahil Azeem (Hausa: Tsarki ya tabbata ga Allah, mai girma da godiyarsa)

Arabic: سبحان الله وبحمده، سبحان الله العظيم

English: Glory be to Allah and praise be to Him, Glory be to Allah the Great
