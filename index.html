Morning Azkar

Subhanallah (Hausa: Tsarki ya tabbata ga Allah)

Arabic: سبحان الله

English: Glory be to Allah

Alhamdulillah (Hausa: Godiya ta tabbata ga Allah)

Arabic: الحمد لله

English: Praise be to Allah

Allahu Akbar (Hausa: Allah ya fi kowa girma)

Arabic: الله أكبر

English: Allah is the Greatest

La ilaha illallah (Hausa: Babu wani abin bautawa da ya cancanta sai Allah)

Arabic: لا إله إلا الله

English: There is no deity but Allah

Astaghfirullah (Hausa: Ina neman gafarar Allah)

Arabic: أستغفر الله

English: I seek forgiveness from Allah

La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah (Hausa: Babu ƙarfin aikata alheri sai tare da Allah)

Arabic: لا حول ولا قوة إلا بالله

English: There is no power or might except with Allah

Bismillah (Hausa: Da sunan Allah)

Arabic: بسم الله

English: In the name of Allah

Subhanallah wa bihamdihi (Hausa: Tsarki ya tabbata ga Allah da godiyarsa)

Arabic: سبحان الله وبحمده

English: Glory be to Allah and praise be to Him

Rabbi ghfirli (Hausa: Ya Ubangiji, ka gafarta mini)

Arabic: ربي اغفر لي

English: My Lord, forgive me

Rabbi zidni ilma (Hausa: Ya Ubangiji, ka ƙara mini ilimi)

Arabic: ربي زدني علما

English: My Lord, increase me in knowledge

Bismillah alladhi la yadurru ma'asmihi shay'un fil ardi wala fissama'i wa huwa assami'u al-'alim

Arabic: بسم الله الذي لا يضر مع اسمه شيء في الأرض ولا في السماء وهو السميع العليم

English: In the name of Allah with whose name nothing on earth or in the heavens can harm, and He is the Hearing, the Knowing.

A'udhu billahi minash shaytanir rajim

Arabic: أعوذ بالله من الشيطان الرجيم

English: I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan

Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa huwa

Arabic: حسبي الله لا إله إلا هو

English: Allah is sufficient for me; there is no deity except Him

La ilaha illa Anta, subhanaka inni kuntu minadhdhalimin

Arabic: لا إله إلا أنت سبحانك إني كنت من الظالمين

English: There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers

Astaghfirullah wa atubu ilayh

Arabic: أستغفر الله وأتوب إليه

English: I seek forgiveness from Allah and repent to Him

Ya Hayyu Ya Qayyum

Arabic: يا حي يا قيوم

English: O Ever-Living, O Sustainer

Raditu billahi Rabban wa bil-Islami dinan wa bi-Muhammadin Nabiyya

Arabic: رضيت بالله ربا وبالإسلام دينا وبمحمد نبيا

English: I am pleased with Allah as my Lord, Islam as my religion, and Muhammad as my Prophet

Allahumma anta rabbi, la ilaha illa anta, khalaqtani wa ana abduka

Arabic: اللهم أنت ربي لا إله إلا أنت خلقتني وأنا عبدك

English: O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no deity except You; You created me, and I am Your servant

Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi, adada khalqihi, wa rida nafsihi, wa zinata arshihi

Arabic: سبحان الله وبحمده عدد خلقه ورضا نفسه وزنة عرشه

English: Glory be to Allah and praise be to Him, in numbers equal to His creation, in a measure equal to His pleasure, and in weight equal to His throne

Allahumma inna nas'aluka 'ilman nafi'an wa rizqan tayyiban wa 'amalan mutaqabbalan

Arabic: اللهم إنا نسألك علماً نافعاً ورزقاً طيباً وعملاً متقبلاً

English: O Allah, we ask You for beneficial knowledge, pure sustenance, and accepted deeds
